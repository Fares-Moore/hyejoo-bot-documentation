Quests and Achievements 🎯

Quests and achievements give you long-term goals to work toward with juicy rewards along the way! Think of quests as your daily, weekly, and monthly to-do list, while achievements are permanent brag-worthy milestones. Ready to stack those rewards? Let's go! 🚀

What's the difference? 🤔​

SystemHow it worksRewards
Quests 🎪Time-limited tasks that refresh daily, weekly, or monthlyTomatoes 🍅, lemons 🍋, vials 🧪, XP
Achievements 🏅Permanent milestones that you unlock onceBigger tomato/lemon/vial payouts

Quests 🎪​

Quests are time-limited objectives you can complete for rewards. They come in three types based on how often they refresh:

TypeDurationTiersExamples
Daily Quests 🗓️Reset every day (on /daily)Tier 1 & 2Claim 4 cards, forge a 1★ card, bid on 2 auctions
Weekly Quests 📅7 days to completeTier 3 & 4Claim 28 cards, complete 4 quests, bid on 12 auctions
Monthly Quests 📆30 days to completeTier 5 & 6Claim 120 cards, complete 15 quests, bid on 30 auctions

Quest commands 🎮​

CommandParametersWhat it does
/quest list(none)Show all your active quests and their progress
/quest infoOptional quest IDView details and tips for a specific quest

Quick tips​

  • Check your quests daily — /quest list shows what's available. Daily quests are quick and easy rewards!
  • Plan around your playstyle — If you forge a lot, the forging quests are free rewards. Same for auctions, claims, and draws.
  • Hidden achievements are fun surprises — Some achievements are hidden until you unlock them. Keep playing and exploring!
  • The star collector chain — The star count achievements (1k → 5k → 10k → 15k → 20k → 25k) are a long-term grind with increasing rewards.
  • Anniversary achievements — The 1-year through 5-year achievements give a random 3★ card! Set a reminder.
  • Use achievements to track goals — /achievements missing:true shows what you still need to work toward.
