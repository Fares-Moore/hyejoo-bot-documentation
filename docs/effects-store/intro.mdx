# Effects and Store

:::note
Tomatoes are great for claiming cards, but the real economy runs on lemons 🍋 — and that's where the store, effects, and plots come in. This section covers everything you can buy, build, craft, and equip to level up your game.
:::

## What's in this section

The bot's economy goes far beyond just claiming cards. Here's what you'll find:

- **🛒 Store and Economy** — Visit the `/store` to browse categories, buy blueprints, effect recipes, and special items using tomatoes 🍅 or lemons 🍋.
- **✨ Effect Cards** — Passive and usable effects that boost your gameplay. Craft them from recipes, equip passives to your hero, and use actives for one-time benefits.
- **🏘️ Guild Plots & Buildings** — Buy plots in your guild, build structures like the Castle, Gacha Bank, Tavern, and more, and earn passive lemon income from other players' actions.
- **🏪 Guild Store** — Server-wide purchases using the guild's balance to build permanent structures that benefit everyone.

## How the economy flows

- **Tomatoes 🍅** — Your main claim currency, earned through daily activities, quests, and achievements.
- **Flakes ❄️** — Reward currency earned mainly from `/daily`. Used for specific store purchases and progression.
- **Vials 🧪** — Earned by liquefying unwanted cards. Used to `/draw` new cards.
- **Lemons 🍋** — The premium-ish currency earned through plots, auctions, and events. Used to buy blueprints, bonus items, and upgrade buildings.
