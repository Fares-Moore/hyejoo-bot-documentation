Store & Economy 🛒

Hyejoo Bot has multiple stores and currencies. This guide covers how to browse, buy, and use items from the store, plus what each currency is for.

Currencies at a glance​

CurrencyIconHow to earnMain uses
Tomatoes❄️Daily activities, quests, achievements, workClaiming cards, buying effect recipes, guild store purchases
Flakes❄️/daily rewardsStore purchases, progressing certain collections
Vials🧪Liquefying unwanted cards/draw to get new cards
Lemons🍋Guild plots, auctions, events, daily castle incomeBuying blueprints, bonus items, upgrading buildings

Store overview​

The store is accessed via /store commands. Items are organized into categories that you can browse by number.

CommandWhat it does
/store viewBrowse store categories and see available items in each.
/store infoView details about a specific item, including price and requirements.
/store buyPurchase an item using the appropriate currency.

How to browse​

  1. Use /store view to see the list of categories (e.g., Blueprints, Effect Recipes, Bonus Items).
  2. Each category is numbered. Use /store view store_number:# to open a category.
  3. Inside a category, items are listed with their ID, name, price, and a short description.
  4. Use /store info itemID:item_id to see full details.
  5. Use /store buy itemID:item_id to purchase.

Purchase limits​

  • Ticket Store items are limited to 3 purchases per day. The counter resets on your next /daily.
  • Some items may require a minimum user level or an active boost event to be available.

Store categories​

Blueprints 🏗️​

Blueprints are used to build structures on your guild plots. Each blueprint costs lemons 🍋 and requires a certain player level to place. Once built, it generates passive lemon income based on other players' actions.

BlueprintPrice 🍋Level ReqWhat it does
Castle15020Required first building. Generates 30 lemons/day and boosts storage for other buildings.
Gacha Bank50030Earns lemons when users in the guild run /daily (lvl 1) or also /claim (lvl 2).
Tavern75040Earns lemons when someone completes an achievement (lvl 1) or quests too (lvl 2).
Smithing Hub1,00050Earns lemons when users forge (lvl 1), liquefy (lvl 2), or draw cards (lvl 3).
Auction House5,000100Earns lemons when someone wins an auction bid. Higher levels give 2x-3x payouts.

See the Guild Plots & Buildings page for full details on building levels and upgrades.

Bonus Items 🎁​

Bonus items are special purchases bought with lemons 🍋 (or Flakes ❄️ for the Legend Swapper) that give unique account-wide upgrades.

Effect Slot Upgrade50,000🍋 Lemons100Adds 1 passive effect slot for 210 days (max 1 per 100 levels).
Effect Use Increase20,000🍋 Lemons100Adds 1 use to an active effect or 3 days to a passive effect.

Guild Store 🏪​

The guild store sells buildings that are purchased using the guild's balance (tomatoes/lemons owned by the server). Only the server owner or designated managers can buy from this store. Purchases are built immediately — they don't go to your inventory.

BuildingPriceCurrencyWhat it does
Lemonade Stand2,500❄️ TomatoesIncreases lemon storage capacity of all user plots in the guild by up to 50% at max level.
Pamper Central15,000❄️ TomatoesReduces maintenance fees for all buildings in the guild by up to 20% at max level.
Discount Center5,000❄️ TomatoesReduces auction listing fees in the guild by up to 20% at max level.

Inventory commands​

Once you buy items, they go into your inventory. Use these commands to manage them:

CommandWhat it does
/inventory listShow all items in your inventory.
/inventory infoView details about a specific inventory item.
/inventory useUse an item (e.g., build a blueprint, craft a recipe, activate a bonus).
/inventory previewPreview what an item does before using it.
/inventory sellSell an inventory item you no longer need.
tip

You can find items by their ID or by number index. Use /inventory list to see your items with their index numbers, then /inventory info index:# for quick details.

Quick tips​

  • Start with a Castle 🏰 — Before you can build any other plot buildings, you need a Castle first. Save up 150 🍋 and buy a Castle blueprint from the store.
  • Check your balance — Use /balance to see all your currencies at a glance.
  • Don't forget the ticket limit — The ticket store resets at your next /daily, so plan your purchases accordingly.
  • Guild store needs permissions — You need to be a server owner or a designated manager to buy guild buildings. Ask your server admin if you think something should be built!
