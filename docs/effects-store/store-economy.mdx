# Store & Economy 🛒

Hyejoo Bot has multiple stores and currencies. This guide covers how to browse, buy, and use items from the store, plus what each currency is for.

## Currencies at a glance

| Currency | Icon | How to earn | Main uses |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Tomatoes | 🍅 | Daily activities, quests, achievements, work | Claiming cards, buying effect recipes, guild store purchases |
| Flakes | ❄️ | `/daily` rewards | Store purchases, progressing certain collections |
| Vials | 🧪 | Liquefying unwanted cards | `/draw` to get new cards |
| Lemons | 🍋 | Guild plots, auctions, events, daily castle income | Buying blueprints, bonus items, upgrading buildings |

## Store overview

The store is accessed via `/store` commands. Items are organized into categories that you can browse by number.

| Command | What it does |
| --- | --- |
| `/store view` | Browse store categories and see available items in each. |
| `/store info` | View details about a specific item, including price and requirements. |
| `/store buy` | Purchase an item using the appropriate currency. |

### How to browse

1. Use `/store view` to see the list of categories (e.g., Blueprints, Effect Recipes, Bonus Items).
2. Each category is numbered. Use `/store view store_number:#` to open a category.
3. Inside a category, items are listed with their ID, name, price, and a short description.
4. Use `/store info itemID:item_id` to see full details.
5. Use `/store buy itemID:item_id` to purchase.

### Purchase limits

- **Ticket Store** items are limited to **3 purchases per day**. The counter resets on your next `/daily`.
- Some items may require a minimum user level or an active boost event to be available.

## Store categories

### Blueprints 🏗️

Blueprints are used to build structures on your guild plots. Each blueprint costs **lemons 🍋** and requires a certain player level to place. Once built, it generates passive lemon income based on other players' actions.

| Blueprint | Price 🍋 | Level Req | What it does |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Castle** | 150 | 20 | Required first building. Generates 30 lemons/day and boosts storage for other buildings. |
| **Gacha Bank** | 500 | 30 | Earns lemons when users in the guild run `/daily` (lvl 1) or also `/claim` (lvl 2). |
| **Tavern** | 750 | 40 | Earns lemons when someone completes an achievement (lvl 1) or quests too (lvl 2). |
| **Smithing Hub** | 1,000 | 50 | Earns lemons when users forge (lvl 1), liquefy (lvl 2), or draw cards (lvl 3). |
| **Auction House** | 5,000 | 100 | Earns lemons when someone wins an auction bid. Higher levels give 2x-3x payouts. |

See the [Guild Plots & Buildings](./guild-plots) page for full details on building levels and upgrades.

### Bonus Items 🎁

Bonus items are special purchases bought with **lemons 🍋** (or tomatoes 🍅 for the Legend Swapper) that give unique account-wide upgrades.

| Item | Price | Currency | Level Req | What it does |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Effect Slot Upgrade** | 50,000 | 🍋 Lemons | 100 | Adds 1 passive effect slot for 210 days (max 1 per 100 levels). |
| **Effect Use Increase** | 20,000 | 🍋 Lemons | 100 | Adds 1 use to an active effect or 3 days to a passive effect. |
| **Legendary Card Swapper** | 10,000,000 | 🍅 Tomatoes | 100 | Swaps a legendary card for another random legendary in the same collection. |

### Guild Store 🏪

The guild store sells buildings that are purchased using the **guild's balance** (tomatoes/lemons owned by the server). Only the **server owner** or designated **managers** can buy from this store. Purchases are built immediately — they don't go to your inventory.

| Building | Price | Currency | What it does |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Lemonade Stand** | 2,500 | 🍅 Tomatoes | Increases lemon storage capacity of all user plots in the guild by up to 50% at max level. |
| **Pamper Central** | 15,000 | 🍅 Tomatoes | Reduces maintenance fees for all buildings in the guild by up to 20% at max level. |
| **Discount Center** | 5,000 | 🍅 Tomatoes | Reduces auction listing fees in the guild by up to 20% at max level. |

## Inventory commands

Once you buy items, they go into your inventory. Use these commands to manage them:

| Command | What it does |
| --- | --- |
| `/inventory list` | Show all items in your inventory. |
| `/inventory info` | View details about a specific inventory item. |
| `/inventory use` | Use an item (e.g., build a blueprint, craft a recipe, activate a bonus). |
| `/inventory preview` | Preview what an item does before using it. |
| `/inventory sell` | Sell an inventory item you no longer need. |

:::tip
You can find items by their ID or by number index. Use `/inventory list` to see your items with their index numbers, then `/inventory info index:#` for quick details.
:::

## Quick tips

- **Start with a Castle** 🏰 — Before you can build any other plot buildings, you need a Castle first. Save up 150 🍋 and buy a Castle blueprint from the store.
- **Check your balance** — Use `/balance` to see all your currencies at a glance.
- **Don't forget the ticket limit** — The ticket store resets at your next `/daily`, so plan your purchases accordingly.
- **Guild store needs permissions** — You need to be a server owner or a designated manager to buy guild buildings. Ask your server admin if you think something should be built!
