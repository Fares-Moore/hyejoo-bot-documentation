# Effect Cards ✨

Effect Cards are special items that give you passive buffs or one-time active abilities. You craft them from the store using **effect recipes**, then equip or use them through your hero.

## How effects work

- **Crafting** — Buy an effect recipe from `/store` (under Effect Recipes), then use it from your inventory. It consumes the required cards from your collection.
- **Passive Effects** — Once crafted, equip passives to your hero's effect slots using `/hero equip`. They provide ongoing buffs as long as they're slotted.
- **Active Effects** — Use them directly with `/effect use`. Each active effect has a limited number of uses and a cooldown between uses.
- **Viewing effects** — Use `/effect list actives` to see your usable effects, or `/effect list passives` (or `/hero slots`) to see your equipped passives.
- **Effect info** — Use `/effect info` to see details about any effect card.

:::note
Your hero starts with **2 passive effect slots** by default. You can unlock more with **Effect Slot Upgrade** items from the bonus store!
:::

## Hero & Effects commands

These are the main commands for managing your hero and effects:

| Command | What it does |
| --- | --- |
| `/hero show` | View your hero and their equipped effect slots. |
| `/hero get` | Set or change your hero (from submitted heroes). |
| `/hero list` | Browse available heroes you can choose. |
| `/hero equip` | Equip a passive effect card to an empty hero slot. |
| `/hero slots` | View your passive effect slots and equipped effects (same as `/effect list passives`). |
| `/effect list actives` | Show your usable active effect cards. |
| `/effect list passives` | Show your passive effect cards. |
| `/effect info` | Get details about a specific effect. |
| `/effect use` | Use an active effect card. |

## Effect slot upgrades

You start with **2 passive effect slots**. To unlock more:

1. Buy an **Effect Slot Upgrade** from the bonus store for 50,000 🍋.
2. You must be at least **level 100** to use it.
3. Each upgrade adds **1 slot** for **210 days**.
4. You can unlock a maximum of **1 slot per 100 levels**.

Use `/hero slots` to see your current slots, their remaining duration, and what effects are equipped.

## Quick tips

- **Spell Card + active effects** — The **Impossible Spell Card** passive reduces ALL active effect cooldowns by 40%. Definitely worth crafting if you use actives often.
- **Festival of Wishes** is relatively cheap (1,000 🍅, 3x 1★ cards) and very handy for auction players — you'll get notified whenever someone lists a card from your wishlist.
- **Use Claim Recall after a big claim session** — If you've claimed a lot of cards and the cost has gone up, Claim Recall brings the count down by 4, saving you tomatoes on your next claim.
- **The Ruler Jeanne** reduces your `/daily` cooldown from 20h to 17h — that's 3 hours saved every day, adding up over time!
