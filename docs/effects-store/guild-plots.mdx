# Guild Plots & Buildings 🏘️

Plots are your **passive lemon income** system. Buy a plot in a guild, build structures on it, and earn lemons 🍋 whenever other players take certain actions in that server.

## Getting started with plots

To start using plots you need to be at least **level 20**. Each plot costs lemons, and the price increases the more plots you own.

### Plot commands

| Command | What it does |
| --- | --- |
| `/plot list` | Show all your plots in the current guild. |
| `/plot list global` | Show all your plots across all guilds. |
| `/plot buy` | Purchase a new plot in the current guild. |
| `/plot info` | View details, stored lemons, and stats for a specific plot. |
| `/plot info global` | View plot info across all guilds. |
| `/plot upgrade` | Upgrade a building on one of your plots to the next level. |
| `/plot collect` | Collect all stored lemons from your plots in the current guild. |
| `/plot demolish` | Demolish a plot and building, getting 75% of invested lemons back. |

### Plot limits

- **Per guild**: Your guild level determines how many plots you can have. The formula is `ceil(guild_level / 8) + 1` plots per guild.
- **Global**: You can own `max(0, floor((player_level - 20) / 10) + 1)` plots total across all guilds.
- **Minimum level**: You need to be **level 20** before you can buy your first plot.

### Plot costs

The cost of buying a new plot doubles with each plot you own:

`25 × 2^(number of plots you already own)`

| Plots owned | Next plot cost 🍋 |
| --- | --- |
| 0 | 25 |
| 1 | 50 |
| 2 | 100 |
| 3 | 200 |
| 4 | 400 |
| 5 | 800 |
| 6 | 1,600 |
| 7 | 3,200 |
| 8 | 6,400 |
| 9 | 12,800 |
| 10 | 25,600 |

### Lemon cap

You have a maximum number of lemons you can hold at once. Your cap is determined by the cost of your next plot:

- If your next plot costs less than 100,000 🍋, your cap is **100,000 🍋**.
- If your next plot costs 100,000 🍋 or more, your cap equals that cost.

If collecting plot revenue would exceed your cap, you'll be warned and can choose to collect anyway (forfeiting the excess) or cancel.

## Buildings (Blueprints) 🏗️

Buildings are placed on plots using **blueprints** bought from the store with lemons 🍋. Each building requires a specific player level to build and has multiple upgrade levels.

### Important building rules

- You **must build a Castle first** before placing any other building in a guild.
- You **cannot have two of the same building** in the same guild.
- Buildings earn passive lemons based on **other players' actions** in the guild — not your own.
- Buildings have **maintenance costs** paid from the guild's balance.

## Storage capacity

Each building has a maximum lemon storage before it stops earning more. The base storage caps are:

| Building Level | Base Storage |
| --- | --- |
| 1 | 300 🍋 |
| 2 | 600 🍋 |
| 3 | 1,200 🍋 |
| 4 | 1,800 🍋 |
| 5 | 3,600 🍋 |
| 6 | 7,200 🍋 |

Your Castle level increases this storage by a percentage. If your guild also has a **Lemonade Stand**, storage is boosted further by 10% per stand level.

## Collecting lemons 🍋

Use `/plot collect` to gather all stored lemons from your plots in a guild. You can collect once per hour.

- If collecting would exceed your lemon cap, the bot will warn you.
- You can choose to continue and forfeit the excess, or cancel.
- Collected lemons go directly into your balance.

## Demolishing plots

If you want to free up a plot slot, use `/plot demolish`:

- **Empty plots** are deleted with no refund.
- **Plots with buildings** give you back **75% of the total lemons** you invested (blueprint price + all upgrade costs).
- If you have multiple buildings and one of them is a **Castle**, you cannot demolish the Castle while other buildings exist in the guild.
- Demolished plots free up a slot so you can build something else.

## Quick tips

- **Castle first, always** 🏰 — You can't build anything else without a Castle on your plot. Make this your first purchase.
- **Choose your guild wisely** — Plots only earn from actions in the guild they're placed in. Active guilds with lots of players will earn you more lemons!
- **Collect regularly** — Buildings have storage caps, so if you don't collect, you'll miss out on potential earnings once they hit the cap.
- **Consider the Lemonade Stand** — If you're in a guild with active builders, a Lemonade Stand boosts everyone's storage, meaning less frequent collecting needed.
- **Level matters** — Many buildings and upgrades are locked behind player levels. Keep playing to unlock higher-tier options!
