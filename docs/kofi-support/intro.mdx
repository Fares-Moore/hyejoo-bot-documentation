# Ko-Fi Support ☕

Love Hyejoo Bot and want to support its development? You can drop a coffee on Ko-Fi to help keep the bot running and unlock some cool perks along the way! This page covers everything you need to know about supporting Hyejoo.

## How to support

1. Head over to our [Ko-Fi page](https://ko-fi.com) and make a donation.
2. Any donation of **$5 or more** grants you **30 days of premium membership**.
3. After your donation, use `/kofi` with your transaction ID or link to verify your payment and claim your perks.
4. Your premium membership will be activated immediately and lasts for the duration purchased.

### Premium membership details

| Aspect | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Minimum donation | $5 USD |
| Premium duration | 30 days per $5 donated |
| Stacking | Multiple donations stack — `$10 = 60 days`, `$15 = 90 days`, etc. |
| Expiry | Premium expires after the purchased duration. You can re-up anytime! |
| Auto-reward | AmuPlus role is automatically added to your account |

:::tip
Don't forget to run `/kofi` with your transaction ID after donating! Your payment needs to be verified before premium kicks in.
:::

### How to verify your payment

After donating on Ko-Fi:

1. Copy your **transaction ID** (txid) from your Ko-Fi order page or the order confirmation URL.
2. Run `/kofi transID:your_transaction_id` in the bot.
3. You'll get a confirmation message with your premium membership duration.
4. You can also paste the full order URL instead of just the ID — the bot will extract the txid automatically.

```
/kofi transID:abc123-abc123-abc123
/kofi transID:https://ko-fi.com/...?txid=abc123-abc123-abc123
```

:::warning
The transaction ID is visible to you when you make the purchase. If you lose it, you can contact us in the [support server] for help.
:::

| Aspect | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Cooldown | 24 hours |
| Requirement | AmuPlus role |

| Command | `/koficlaim` |

Every 24 hours, you can claim a random lottery card. These are special cards from the `lottery` collection that aren't available through regular claiming!

| Aspect | Details |

## FAQ ❓

### How do I become a premium member?
Donate **$5 or more** on our Ko-Fi page, then run `/kofi transID:your_transaction_id` in the bot to verify.

### What does premium give me?
Premium grants you an **AmuPlus role**, enhanced profile display features, the ability to mark cards with emoji, and access to premium preferences.

### How long does premium last?
**30 days per $5 donated**. If you donate $15, you get 90 days. Donations stack!

### What's the difference between `/koficlaim` and `/kofipromo`?
- `/koficlaim` is a **daily** claim (24h cooldown) that gives a random **Lottery Collection** card.
- `/kofipromo` is a **monthly** claim (30d cooldown) that gives a card from a **tier-based promo collection** with weighted rarities.

### My donation isn't working with `/kofi`. What do I do?
Make sure you're using the correct transaction ID from your Ko-Fi order page. You can either paste the full URL or just the txid parameter. If it still doesn't work, reach out in the [support server] for help.

### Do I need to keep my Ko-Fi subscription active?
Premium is time-based — once activated, it lasts for the duration you paid for regardless of subscription status.

### Can I upgrade my tier later?
Yes! Higher donations just add more time or unlock higher tiers. Each new donation can push you into a higher tier for better monthly promo chances.
