Collection Progress 📋

Tracking, completing, and resetting collections is the core of clouting. This page covers all the commands for managing your collection progress.

/collection list — Your progress at a glance​

Shows your collection completion status across all collections or a filtered subset.

ParameterWhat it does
colsFilter by specific collection names or IDs
completedShow only completed (true) or incomplete (false) collections
cloutedShow only clouted (true) or unclouted (false) collections
sortCompleteSort by completion status

Completion logic​

  • Normal collections: All cards in the set must be owned.
  • Fragment collections: Only 1★ cards need to be owned (higher stars come from fragment merging).

/collection info — Collection details​

View detailed info about a specific collection, including all cards in the set and your ownership status.

ParameterWhat it does
collectionName or ID of the collection to inspect
fields shownCard names, star levels, whether you own them

/collection reset — Reset completed collections​

When you've completed a collection and want to reset it for additional rewards or progression.

ParameterWhat it does
collectionThe completed collection to reset
ShowsRequirements and rewards for resetting

Reset requirements​

  • You must own every card in the collection.
  • Some collections have specific reset conditions.
  • Resetting may consume cards or require additional resources.

Collection types​

Understanding different collection types helps you plan your clouting:

TypeHow to completeNotes
NormalCollect all cards in the setStandard completion
FragmentsCollect all 1★ cardsHigher stars come from fragment system
PromoLimited-time event collectionsOnly available during events
AlbumSpecial album collectionsHave their own rules for forging
LotteryRare lottery collectionsSpecial filtering rules apply
Legendary (5★)Collect all 5★ cardsRarest cards in the game
Limited/BirthdaysTime-limited special cardsMay not be re-released

The clout system​

When you complete a collection:

  1. You can clout it — marking it as completed and adding to your total clout count.
  2. Use /collection list clouted:true to show only collections you've clouted.
  3. Use /collection list clouted:false to see collections still waiting to be completed.
  4. Use /collection list completed:true to see which sets have all cards collected.

Clouted collections​

The clouted filter in /collection list shows the status of your clout progress:

  • clouted:true — Collections you've already completed and clouted
  • clouted:false — Collections you haven't yet completed (or completed but not yet clouted)

Quick tips​

  • Filter by completion — Use /collection list completed:false to see what collections still need work and prioritize your claims.
  • Focus on one collection at a time — Pick a collection and claim cards from it to make steady progress.
  • Check collection info often — /collection info shows you exactly which cards you're missing.
  • Don't forget to clout — After completing a collection, make sure to actually clout it to mark your progress!
