# Clouts and Progression 🏆

Clouts are how you show off your collection! In Hyejoo, "clouting" means completing card collections, ranking up your cards, and progressing through the game's many systems. This section covers everything related to growing your account and flexing your achievements.

## What is clouting?

Clouting is the main endgame loop. When you complete a card collection (get every card in a set), you earn the right to **clout** it — marking it as completed and showing off your achievement. The more collections you complete, the more clout you earn.

## Progression systems at a glance

| System | What it does | Key commands |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **Daily Claims** | Your daily card claiming loop with scaling costs | `/daily`, `/claim cards`, `/level claim` |
| **Card Training** | Level up your cards through experience gain | `/train` |
| **Ascension** | Combine two maxed cards into a higher-star card | `/ascend` |
| **The Smith System** | Forge, liquefy, combine, and draw for new cards | `/forge`, `/combine`, `/liquefy`, `/draw` |
| **Legendary Rolling** | Use legendary tickets for a guaranteed 5★ | `/legroll` |
| **Collection Progress** | Track, complete, and reset your collections | `/collection list`, `/collection info`, `/collection reset` |
| **Quests & Achievements** | Long-term goals with bonus rewards | `/quest list`, `/achievements`, `/miss` |

## Quick tips

- **Plan your daily loop** — Start with `/daily` to refresh your state, then use `/claim cards` for your main pulls.
- **Train regularly** — Maxed cards are needed for ascension, so keep training your best cards.
- **Save for legendary tickets** — `/legroll` gives you a guaranteed 5★, one of the best paths to rare cards.
- **Check collection progress often** — Use `/collection list` to see what you still need and prioritize your claims.
