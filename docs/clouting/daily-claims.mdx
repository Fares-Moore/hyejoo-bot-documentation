# Daily Claims 🗓️

Your daily claim loop is the bread and butter of progression. This covers getting your daily reward, claiming cards, and understanding how costs scale.

## `/daily` — Your daily reward

Use `/daily` to claim your daily flakes and refresh your daily state. This is the most important habit in the game because it gives you your main currency and resets your claim cost.

| Aspect | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Cooldown | 20 hours (17 hours with **The Ruler Jeanne** passive effect) |
| Base reward | 500 ❄️ |
| Streak bonus | +5 ❄️ for each consecutive streak day |
| What resets | Claim cost, daily quests, store purchase limits |

:::tip
Make `/daily` your first stop every time you log in. It refreshes everything and sets you up for the day.
:::

## `/claim cards` — Building your collection

This is how you get new cards. You spend **tomatoes 🍅** to claim random cards from the pool. The more you claim in a day, the more each claim costs.

### How claim cost works

Claim costs scale up the more cards you claim in a single day. The cost resets when you use `/daily`.

| Claims in a day | Approximate cost trend |
| --- | --- |
| First claim | Low cost (starts at 50 ❄️) |
| Incremental claims | Increases by 50 ❄️ for every claimed card |
| 30 claims | **Guarantees a promo card drop during event boost periods** |

### Optional parameters for `/claim cards`

| Parameter | What it does |
| --- | --- |
| `count` | Number of cards to claim (max 10 per command) |
| `boost` | Apply an active boost ID for special card drops |
| `group_type` | Filter by group type (e.g., girlgroups, boygroups) |
| `promo` (deprecated) | Use promo currency during special events |
| `any` (deprecated) | Bypass guild lock restrictions |

:::tip
Check your balance with `/balance` before claiming to avoid overspending. Claim in smaller batches to keep costs manageable.
:::

### Special claim bonuses

- **Tohru effect** — If you have **Gift From Tohru** passive equipped and haven't claimed any cards today, your first claim has increased chance at a 3★ card.
- **Boost drops** — During active boosts, every 30th claim guarantees a boost card drop.

## `/level claim` — Level-based claiming

An alternate claim flow that uses level-based logic instead of standard claim mechanics. This can give different card results compared to `/claim cards`.

| Aspect | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Parameters | Same optional params as `/claim cards` (count, promo, any, boost, group_type) |
| Best for | Players who want card levels distribution rather than regular card claiming |
