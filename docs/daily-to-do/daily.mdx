# Daily

## /daily

Claim your daily flakes once every 20 hours, or every 17 hours if the Ruler Jeanne effect is active. This is one of the most important habits in the game because it gives you your main currency, refreshes your daily state, and helps you stay on top of quests and events.

### What you get

- A base reward of 500❄️
- An extra 5❄️ for each streak day you keep up
- A fresh daily summary that may include events, boosts, announcements, or pending trades

:::tip
If you are trying to build momentum, make /daily your first stop every time you log in.
:::
