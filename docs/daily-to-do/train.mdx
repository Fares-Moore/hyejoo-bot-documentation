# Train

## /train

Train a card to earn experience points and help it level up. This is part of the progression loop for card growth, and it becomes more important once you start collecting stronger cards or aiming for ascension.

### What to know

- The command has a 15-minute cooldown
- Training is more useful when you already have cards you want to keep improving
- It pairs well with /cards and /profile when you want to see how your collection is growing
