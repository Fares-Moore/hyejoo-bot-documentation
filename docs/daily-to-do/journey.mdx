# Journey

## /journey

Head out on a short journey and return with a random card. The command has a 12-hour cooldown, so it is best used as a steady progress tool rather than a spam action.

### Why players use it

- It gives you a chance at cards from different collections
- It works well as a low-pressure way to keep collecting
- It can be a nice supplement to /daily and /work when you are waiting on other progress

:::note
Journeys are fun, but they are not guaranteed to give rare cards. Think of them as a bonus layer of progression rather than your main source of pulls.
:::
