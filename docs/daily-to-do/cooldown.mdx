# Cooldown

## /cooldown

Use this command to see when your main actions are ready again. It is especially helpful for tracking /daily, /work, /train, /journey, and Ko-Fi claim timers.

### Why it helps

- Prevents accidental spam
- Helps you plan your routine
- Makes it easier to decide whether to wait, claim, or train
