# Daily To-Dos

This section focuses on the commands that players use most often as part of their day-to-day routine. If you want the broader overview of the bot, its goals, and its economy, head to the getting-started pages first.

## A simple daily loop

A good beginner routine usually looks like this:

1. Use /daily to claim your daily flakes and refresh your daily state.
2. Check /balance so you know how much you can spend.
3. Use /work for quick rewards while you are waiting on other timers.
4. Try /journey when it is ready for a chance at a new card.
5. Check /cooldown to see what is ready next.

## Common daily commands

These are the commands most players will return to again and again:

| Command | What it does |
| --- | --- |
| /daily | Claims your daily reward and refreshes your daily progress |
| /balance | Shows your current currency and claim readiness |
| /work | Gives you a short, repeatable reward loop |
| /journey | Gives you a chance at a card from a journey reward |
| /cooldown | Shows when your main commands are ready again |
