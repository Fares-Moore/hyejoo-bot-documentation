# Daily To-Dos

### What are these?
Daily To-Dos are the common commands that players will do to progress in Hyejoo, go into any of our `#krpg` channels under the `Community & Play` tab in the Hyejoo Support Discord server to run commands and see what others are up to!
- #krpg
- #krpg2
- #krpg3

### What is the main objective of the game?
It's a sandbox, you could do anything including:
 - Completing card collections of your favourite idols/groups for clouts
 - Collecting as many rare cards as possible just as a flex 
 - Participate in regularly updated community events, interact with other community members and just have fun!
