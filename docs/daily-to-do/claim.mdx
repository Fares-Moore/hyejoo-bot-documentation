# Claiming Cards

## /claim cards

This is how you start building your collection. You spend flakes to claim random cards from the pool, and you can also use boost IDs or promo currency during special events for better chances at specific cards.

### Beginner notes

- The more you claim in a day, the more each claim usually costs
- /level claim is a related option that uses a slightly different flow
- Claiming is usually where your economy and collection goals meet, so it is worth checking /balance before you spend

:::tip
If you are trying to avoid overspending, check your balance first and claim in smaller batches.
:::
