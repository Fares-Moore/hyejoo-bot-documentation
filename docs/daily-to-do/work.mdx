# Work

## /work

Earn some resources every 10 minutes. Sometimes things go wrong and you come back empty handed, but most of the time you'll walk away with flakes, vials, or some user XP.

### Why it is useful

- It is a simple way to make steady progress while you are waiting on other cooldowns
- It helps keep your economy moving even on slower days
- It is one of the easiest commands to fit into a daily routine

:::tip
Use /work when you want a low-effort way to earn while you are doing other things in Discord.
:::
