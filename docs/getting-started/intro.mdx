# Basic Info

## What Is Hyejoo Bot?
Hyejoo is a K-Pop gacha card collecting game where you collect, flex, and complete your favourite kpop groups’ and soloists’ card collections. The bot is the Discord-based tool that helps you manage your cards, currencies, quests, and progression in one place.

## What is the main objective of the game?
It is a sandbox, so you can choose your own pace and playstyle:
- Complete card collections of your favourite idols or groups for clout
- Collect as many rare cards as possible as a flex
- Join regularly updated community events and interact with other players

## How the game usually flows

Most players start with a simple loop:
1. Check /balance to see what resources are available
2. Claim /daily for your daily reward
3. Use /work or /journey for steady progress
4. Spend your resources on cards, training, or other progression goals

That loop is the foundation of the daily-to-do section, while the later pages cover the bot’s economy, commands, and more advanced systems.
