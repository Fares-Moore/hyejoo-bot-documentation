# Balance & Currencies

## Balance
`/balance`
Shows how much currency you currently have — flakes, vials, and lemons. It also tells you how much your next card claim will cost and roughly how many cards you can afford right now.

## Why currencies matter
Currencies are the backbone of the bot’s economy. They decide what you can claim, what you can draw, and how far you can progress in your collection and guild-related activities.

## Flakes (❄️)
Flakes are the main currency used for claiming cards, selling cards, auctioning and bidding on cards, buying blueprints and recipes, and maintaining a guild.

## Vials (🍷)
Vials are used for drawing and liquefying cards. You also earn them through forging and quests, and they are an important part of the card progression loop.

## Lemons (🍋)
Lemons are used for buying plots, blueprints, and recipes in the store. They are earned through quests and from buildings that have been built on plots.
