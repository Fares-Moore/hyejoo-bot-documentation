# Balance & Currencies

## Balance
`/balance`
hows how much currency you currently have — flakes, vials, and lemons. Also tells you how much your next card claim will cost and roughly how many cards you can afford right now.

## Flakes (❄️) 
Flakes are the main currency used and are used for claiming cards, selling cards, auctioning and bidding on cards, buying blueprints (buildings) and recipes (effects) and also what is used when maintaining a guild (your server).

## Vials (🍷) 
Vials are the currency used when drawing and liquefying cards. You use vials when drawing, and essentially use them to acquire cards. You gain vials from liquefying cards, forging and quests.

## Lemons (🍋) 
Lemons are a currency used for buying plots, blueprints and recipes in the store. They are earned via quests and from buildings that have been built on plots.
