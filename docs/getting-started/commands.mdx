# Interacting with the bot

## Commands
To interact with the bot, use slash commands such as /daily, /balance, and /claim cards. The commands are organized around a simple flow: check your resources, claim rewards, grow your collection, and then use your cards or currency for progression.

### Common commands
These are the commands you will see most often as you get started:

- /balance — check your current currencies and claim readiness
- /daily — claim your daily reward
- /work — earn a repeatable reward loop
- /journey — try for a random reward card
- /claim cards — start building your collection
- /cooldown — see when your main actions are ready again

These commands are covered in more detail in the daily-to-do section, while the later pages explain the economy and other progression systems.
