# Limited-Time Activities ⏰

Beyond seasonal events that run on a regular cadence, Hyejoo Bot also hosts special one-off activities that add unique ways to get cards and rewards! These are time-limited features with their own mechanics.

## 1★ Promo Drops 🌟

Sometimes, special 1★ promo cards are activated as **boost drops** that appear randomly in regular claims **without needing a boost ID**. These are different from regular promos — they can show up in any `/claim cards` session as a surprise bonus!

### How they work

- A **boost** is active for a limited time with special cards assigned to it.
- During the boost, regular claims have a chance to drop these special cards.
- Every **30th claim** during an active boost **guarantees** a boost card drop! 🎉
- The cards have a `🅱` (boost) marker to indicate they're special drops.
- You can check active boosts with `/boost list`.

### Example: Ive X Le Sserafim (ivesserafim) 💋

A past 1★ promo event where special cards from these groups could drop randomly during claims. These cards had a unique `ivesserafim` collection tag and featured limited-edition card art.

### How to maximize boost drops

- Claim in larger batches — the 30th claim guarantee means more claims = more boost drops.
- Use `/daily` first to reset your claim cost, then claim as much as you can.
- Check `/boost list` to see which boosts are currently active and what cards are available.

## Anniversary Events 🥂

Hyejoo Bot's anniversary celebrations are special events with unique mechanics, exclusive cards, and limited-time recipes!

### How anniversary events work

- A special **anniversary collection** (e.g., `anniversary26` for the 6th anniversary) is created with exclusive cards.
- A limited-time **Hyejoo Anniversary** effect recipe becomes available in the store for **5,000 🍅**.
- The recipe requires **3× 2★ cards** to craft, and grants **1 use** of the anniversary effect.
- When used, the effect gives you **1 random card** from the anniversary collection.
- You can craft multiple recipes for multiple chances at anniversary cards!

### Anniversary recipe details

| Aspect | Details |
| --- | --- |
| Recipe name | **Hyejoo Anniversary** 🥂 |
| Price | 5,000 🍅 |
| Cards required | 3× 2★ cards |
| Uses per craft | 1 use |
| Cooldown | None |
| Collection | Anniversary collection (e.g., `anniversary26`) |

:::note
Previous anniversaries have also run. For example, `anniversary21` was the 1st anniversary celebration with its own unique cards and rewards!
:::

## Ko-Fi Monthly Rewards ☕

Supporters of Hyejoo Bot on Ko-Fi can claim exclusive promo cards from limited collections each month!

### How monthly Ko-Fi rewards work

- **Ko-Fi tiers** (Amu3, Amu6, Amu9, Amu12, Amu15, Amu30) determine which collections you can pull from.
- Higher tiers have better chances at rarer collections.
- Collections are categorized by rarity:
  - **Common** — Earlier/standard promo collections
  - **Rare** — More recent exclusive collections
  - **Epic** — Premium seasonal collections
  - **Legendary** — Rare older promo collections
- Each tier has weighted chances for each rarity category.

### Collection tiers and weights

| Tier | Common | Rare | Epic | Legendary |
| --- | --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **Amu3** ($3) | 100% | — | — | — |
| **Amu6** ($6) | 55% | 28% | 13% | 4% |
| **Amu9** ($9) | 40% | 32% | 20% | 8% |
| **Amu12** ($12) | 25% | 33% | 28% | 14% |
| **Amu15** ($15) | 15% | 25% | 35% | 25% |
| **Amu30** ($30) | 5% | 15% | 35% | 45% |

## Other limited-time features

### Pets & Companions Event 🐾

- Event ID: `petsevent`
- Special collection with companion-themed cards
- These cards couldn't be combined or used in smith recipes

### Signed Cards 💫

- Event ID: `signed`
- Special signed edition cards from various artists
- Limited-time availability

### Live Auctions 💎

- Event ID: `liveauction`
- Special auction events where exclusive cards are put up for bid
- These cards have a unique `💎` rarity marker

## Commands for limited activities

| Command | What it does |
| --- | --- |
| `/boost list` | Show currently active boosts and their card pools |
| `/claim cards count:N` | Claim multiple cards (more claims = more boost chances) |
| `/store buy itemID:hyejooanniv` | Purchase the anniversary recipe (only during anniversary) |
| `/inventory use` | Use crafted anniversary effect cards |
| `/koficlaim` | Claim your monthly Ko-Fi reward (AmuPlus role required, 24h cooldown) |
| `/kofipromo` | Claim monthly Ko-Fi promo card (AmuPlus role required, 30d cooldown) |

## Quick tips

- **Check boosts often** — Use `/boost list` to see what's active so you don't miss out on special drops.
- **30-claim rhythm** — If a boost is active, the 30th claim of the day is a guaranteed special drop. Plan your claims!
- **Anniversary recipes stack** — You can craft multiple Hyejoo Anniversary recipes during the event period for more chances at exclusive cards.
- **Go higher for better odds** — Higher Ko-Fi tiers give significantly better chances at rare seasonal and legendary collections.
