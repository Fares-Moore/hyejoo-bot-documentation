# Events and Activities 🎉

Hyejoo Bot keeps things fresh with a rotating lineup of events and activities! From seasonal promo drops to limited-time challenges and community giveaways, there's always something happening. This section covers everything you need to know about the bot's event system.

## What's in this section

- **Seasonal Events 🎄🎃🌸** — Limited-time promo collections that appear during holidays and special seasons. Claim exclusive cards before they're gone!
- **Limited-Time Activities ⏰** — Special one-off events like 1★ promo drops, anniversary celebrations, and other time-limited features.
- **Community Events 🌐** — Games, giveaways, and other community-driven events organized by server admins and the Hyejoo team.

## How events work

- **Promo Collections** — Many events add special **promo collections** (marked with `promo: true`) that you can claim from using the boost parameter during the event period.
- **Boost Drops** — During active boosts, every **30th claim** guarantees a special promo card drop.
- **Event Currency** — Some events introduce a temporary currency you earn by claiming promo cards, which can be used to claim even more event cards.
- **Limited Recipes** — Special event-exclusive effect recipes (like **Hyejoo Anniversary**) become available in the store only during the event.
- **Expiration** — Promo collections have an `expires` date. After that, the cards can no longer be claimed.

## Quick tips

- **Check active promos** — Use `/claim cards boost:promo` during active events to get special promo cards alongside your regular claims.
- **30 claims = boost drop** — During an active boost event, your 30th claim of the day guarantees a boost card drop. Plan your claims!
- **Event currency adds up** — Each promo claim earns you event currency. The more you claim, the more you can spend on extra event cards.
- **Don't miss expiration dates** — Keep an eye on event end dates. Once a promo collection expires, those cards are gone (until the next event!).
