Card Management Commands 🛠️

info

This page is your full reference for managing, organizing, comparing, and evaluating your cards. All commands listed here are verified against the bot's command schema (COMMAND_SCHEMA.md).

Core collection commands​

CommandRequired paramsPermissionsWhat it does
/cardsCard query (optional)Any userBrowse the cards you own. Use global:true to search the full card pool. Supports filtering by name, collection, star level, and more.
/summonCard queryAny userSummon and view a specific card from your collection.
/infoCard queryAny userShow card data, owner count, demand (wishlist count), tags, and pricing info for a card.
/claim cardsOptional count/promo/any/boost ID/group typeAny userClaim cards using flakes. Cost scales with number of claims.
/level claimOptional count/promo/any/boost ID/group typeAny userAlternate claims for cards with random amounts of levels.

Comparison and checking commands​

CommandRequired paramsPermissionsWhat it does
/hasTarget user ID + card queryAny userCheck whether another player owns a specific card.
/diff fromTarget user IDAny userCompare your card collection against another user's cards.
/diff forTarget user IDAny userCompare another user's collection against yours.
/profileTarget user ID (optional)Any userView a profile-style overview for a player.
/statsNoneAny userShow your current user statistics.

Wishlist, favorites, and personal organization​

CommandRequired paramsPermissionsWhat it does
/wish oneCard queryAny userAdd one card to your wishlist 📋.
/wish manyCard query, optional countAny userAdd multiple cards to your wishlist.
/wish remove oneCard queryAny userRemove one wishlist entry.
/wish remove manyCard query, optional countAny userRemove multiple wishlist entries.
/fav oneCard queryAny userMark one card as a favorite ⭐.
/fav manyCard query, optional countAny userMark multiple cards as favorites.
/fav remove oneCard queryAny userRemove a favorite mark from one card.
/fav remove manyCard query, optional countAny userRemove favorite marks from multiple cards.

Evaluation and rating commands​

CommandRequired paramsPermissionsWhat it does
/eval oneCard queryAny userEvaluate one card's worth.
/eval manyCard query, optional countAny userEvaluate multiple cards at once.
/rate oneCard queryAny userRate one card.
/rate manyCard query, optional countAny userRate multiple cards.
/rate remove oneCard queryAny userRemove a rating from one card.
/rate remove manyCard query, optional countAny userRemove ratings from multiple cards.

Locking and inventory protection​

CommandRequired paramsPermissionsWhat it does
/lock oneCard queryAny userLock one card in your inventory 🔒.
/lock manyCard query, optional countAny userLock multiple cards.
/lock remove oneCard queryAny userUnlock one card.
/lock remove manyCard query, optional countAny userUnlock multiple cards.
tip

Favoriting cards protects them from accidental selling, liquefying, or use in forges. Favourite your legendaries and rares first!

tip

Locking cards protects them from other players from seeing them when they use /has or /diff from. Use this to hide your cards if you're feeling sneaky...

A simple starter workflow​

If you're new, here's a tidy little flow:

  1. Use /cards to browse your collection.
  2. Use /claim cards to add new cards (or /summon to look up specific ones).
  3. Use /wish to save cards you want later.
  4. Use /eval to understand value before selling or converting.
  5. Use /rate and /fav to keep your collection organized and personal.
  6. Use /lock to protect your most valuable cards.
