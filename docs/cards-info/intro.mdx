# Cards and Collection Overview 🃏

:::note
Cards are the heart of the bot's collecting experience. Whether you're chasing a favorite idol, building a complete set, or just showing off your best pulls, this section is your cozy guide to the collection side of Hyejoo.
:::

## What cards are for

Cards are your main progression pieces. You can:

- **Collect** cards from claims, draws, and special events in the community
- **Compare** your collection with other players and flex your cards!
- **Organize** with favorites, ratings, wishlists, and marks
- **Track** rarities and special card groups
- **Progress** by training, ascending, and completing collections to get 5* Legendary Cards

## Card types at a glance

| Type | Star range | How to get | Rarity |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Normal | 1★ – 4★ | `/claim cards`, `/journey` | Common to Uncommon |
| Legendaries | 5★ | `/legroll` | Clouts 🌟 |
| Fragments | 1★ – 4★ | `/claim cards`, `/journey` | Common to Uncommon  |
| Promos | 1★ – 3★ | `/claim cards` during boost events | Event-limited 🎉 |
| Limited & Birthdays | 4★ | Community Events, through auctions and giveaways | Very Rare ⏰ |
| Live Auction | 4★ | Won via Live Auction Events  | One of a kind |
| Specials | 4* | Community Events, Giveaways, 4* forges | Rare |

## A good collection loop

1. **Check** your current cards with `/cards` (supports card queries and filters!)
2. **Add** new ones with `/claim cards` or `/level claim`
3. **Show off** your cards with `/summon`
4. **Organize** with `/fav`, `/rate`, `/wish`, or `/lock`
5. **Compare** card ownership with `/diff from` or `/diff for`
6. **Evaluate** with `/eval` before selling or converting

## Quick tips

- Use `/cards` to browse your collection with optional card queries and the `global` flag for searching the full pool.
- Use `/wish` to track cards you want to pull later.
- Use `/eval` before turning cards into resources (selling, liquefying, etc.).
- Use `/rate` and `/fav` to personalize your collection and keep your faves easy to find.
- Use `/lock` to protect valuable cards from accidental actions.
