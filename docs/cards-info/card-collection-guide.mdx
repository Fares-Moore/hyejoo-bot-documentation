# Card Collection Guide 🖼️

:::tip
Think of your card collection as a little gallery of your favorite idols and moments. Some players chase shiny stars, while others focus on completing specific sets or collecting special event cards.
:::

## Normal cards (1★–4★) ⭐

These are the everyday cards that make up the bulk of most collections. They form the backbone of your collection progress.

| Star level | Rarity | Claim rate | Used for |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| 1★ | Common | ~47% | Fodder for combines, liquefying |
| 2★ | Common | ~47% | Fodder for combines, liquefying, effect recipes |
| 3★ | Uncommon | ~5% | Core collection pieces, ascension material |
| 4★ | Rare | ~1% | Valuable collection pieces, high-value ascension |

### How to get them

- `/claim cards` — Main source of normal cards
- `/daily` — Base reward and claim resets
- `/work` — Earn flakes to fund claims
- `/journey` — Random card chance every 12 hours
- `/draw` — Spend vials for a chance at cards up to 3★ (or 6★ with **Walpurgis Night** effect)

### What to do with duplicates

- **Combine/Forge** — Turn two cards of the same star level into a new card
- **Liquefy** — Convert into vials 🧪 for `/draw`
- **Sell** — Sell for tomatoes 🍅
- **Effect recipes** — Many recipes require sacrificing specific card rarities

## Legendaries (5★) 🌟

Legendary cards are the big dream pulls — the rarest cards in the game. They often become the centerpiece of a player's collection.

### How to get legendaries

| Method | Details |
| --- | --- |
| **Claim drop** | Each `/claim cards` has a very small RNG-based chance at a 5★ |
| **`/legroll`** | Spend a **Legendary Card Claim Ticket** for a guaranteed 5★ |
| **Special events** | Events and giveaways sometimes award legendary cards |
| **Auction** | Win a legendary from `/auction` |
| **Collections** | Some collection completions reward legendaries |

### Legendary cards in gameplay

- Cannot be used in `/forge` or `/combine`
- Can be ascended (2 legendaries → higher) but requires strategic planning
- Use `/eval` to check a legendary card's value
- Protect your legendaries with `/lock` and `/fav`!
- The **Legendary Card Swapper** (10,000,000 🍅 in the bonus store) lets you swap a legendary for another random one in the same collection

## Fragments 🧩

Fragment collections work differently from normal collections. They use a fragment system where you collect base fragments and upgrade them.

### How fragments work

- Fragment collections focus on collecting the **1★ base cards**
- Higher star cards in fragment collections come from merging/upgrading fragments
- When checking collection completion (`/collection list`), only 1★ cards are counted for fragment collections
- Fragment cards cannot be used in forging

## Special cards ✨

Special cards are tied to events, mechanics, or unique collection goals. These are less common and more memorable.

### Where to find special cards

- **Special collections** — Collections marked with special properties (rarity, lottery, etc.)
- **Event rewards** — Given out during community events
- **Milestone rewards** — Achievements, quests, or level milestones

### Special collection properties

Some collections have special flags that affect gameplay. These flags determine which cards can be used in `/forge`, `/combine`, and other progression commands:

| Property | What it means |
| --- | --- |
| `lottery` | Lottery-sourced cards, special forge rules |
| `album` | Album cards with their own combine/forge behavior |
| `fragments` | Fragment-based progression |
| `liveauction` | Sourced from live auction wins |
| `jackpot` | Jackpot event cards |
| `birthdays` | Birthday celebration cards |
| `limited` | Limited-time only, may never return |
| `signed` | Signed/autograph variant cards |
| `subunitdiscontinued` | Discontinued subunit collections |

## Promo cards 🎉

Promo cards are tied to promotional or limited-release content. They're especially valuable for showing off rare parts of your collection.

### How promos work

- Acquired during **promotional events** using event-specific currency
- Use `/claim cards promo:true` during an active promotion
- Promo card costs use the event currency instead of tomatoes
- During promotions, claiming regular cards gives you bonus promo currency (25% of cost)
- Promo collections have their own forging rules — two promo cards from the same collection can forge into another promo

### Promo restrictions

- Cannot forge promo cards with non-promo cards
- Some effects and recipes are only available during specific promotions (marked with `boostRequired` in the store)

## Limited & Birthday cards ⏰

These cards celebrate special moments, seasonal themes, or celebratory events. They are time-sensitive and often highly sought after.

### Characteristics

- **Limited** — Available for a limited window, may never return
- **Birthdays** — Released for specific idol birthdays
- **Anniversary** — Released for bot anniversary celebrations (e.g., Hyejoo Anniversary26 collection)
- Cannot be used in forging (excluded collection type)

### Collecting tips

- Check `/boost list` to see if any limited events are running
- Limited cards often have higher trade value
- Don't liquefy or sell limited cards — they may be gone forever!

## Live Auction cards 🏛️

Live auction cards are tied to the auction economy. These cards change hands through bidding.

### Getting auction cards

- Browse active auctions with `/auction list`
- Bid on cards with `/auction bid`
- Win the auction to add the card to your collection

### Auction restrictions in forging

- Cards from `liveauction` flagged collections cannot be used in `/forge`
- They can normally be sold, traded, or kept as collection pieces

## Miscellaneous cards 🎲

This category covers cards that don't fit neatly into the main groups — seasonal cards, giveaway rewards, and other special drops.

### Examples

- **Event rewards** — Given out during community activities
- **Giveaway cards** — Distributed through promotional giveaways
- **Milestone cards** — Rewarded for reaching certain goals
- **Legacy cards** — From past systems or discontinued mechanics

## How to grow your collection

A good collection routine usually looks like:

1. **Claim daily** — Start with `/daily`, then `/claim cards` for new pulls
2. **Browse and organize** — Use `/cards` to see what you have, `/wish` to track targets
3. **Evaluate** — Use `/eval` to know what's worth keeping vs selling
4. **Compare** — Use `/diff from` or `/diff for` to benchmark against other players
5. **Progress** — Train, ascend, and work toward collection completion

## Quick tips

- **1★ and 2★ cards** are great for liquefying into vials (especially with **The Holy Grail** effect active).
- **3★ and 4★ cards** are your main collection pieces — keep them for collection completion.
- **Legendaries** are irreplaceable — lock and favorite them immediately.
- **Promo and Limited cards** are time-sensitive — grab them while you can!
- Check `/collection list` regularly to track your progress.
