# Card Commands Guide

## `claim cards`
This is how you get new cards! Spend your flakes to claim random cards from the pool. You can claim multiple at once, use a boost to target specific cards, or spend promo currency during events for special event cards. The more you claim in a day, the more each claim costs — so pace yourself!

---

## `level claim`
A special way to claim cards using your user XP instead of flakes. Great if you're sitting on a lot of XP and want to put it to use.

---

## `claim history`
Shows you a history of cards you've previously claimed, so you can look back at what you've gotten.

---

## `claim info`
Look up details about a specific card in the global card pool — useful if you want to know more about a card before going after it.

---

## `summon`
Summon a card you own to view its image and details. A quick way to show off a card or check what it looks like!

---

## `cards global`
Browse all cards in the entire global card pool — not just the ones you own. Great for exploring what's out there or searching for a specific card.

---

## `sell one`
Sell a single card — either to the bot for flakes, or to another player. If selling to another player, they'll get a prompt to accept or decline. If they don't respond, it becomes a pending transaction they can action later.

---

## `sell many`
Sell a bunch of cards at once, either to the bot or to another player. You'll see a paginated list of the cards being sold before confirming. 

---

## `sell preview`
Want to know how much your cards are worth before actually selling them? This shows you a price breakdown of the cards matching your query without committing to anything.

---

## `eval one`
Check how much a specific card is worth in flakes and vials. Handy before selling or trading!

---

## `eval many`
Check the combined value of a bunch of your cards at once based on a query.

---

## `eval many global`
Same as `eval many` but searches the global card pool instead of just your own cards.

---

## `fav one`
Mark a single card as a favourite. Favourited cards are protected from being accidentally sold in bulk operations.

---

## `fav many`
Mark a whole bunch of cards as favourites at once based on a query. Comes with a confirmation prompt so you don't accidentally favourite things you didn't mean to.

---

## `fav remove one`
Remove the favourite mark from a single card.

---

## `fav remove many`
Remove the favourite mark from multiple cards at once. You can filter by duplicates or limit how many get unfavourited.

---

## `boost list`
See all the currently active boosts — these let you target specific cards when claiming. Shows what's available and how long each boost lasts.

---

## `boost info`
Get the full details on a specific boost, including the complete list of cards you can get from it and how to use it when claiming.

---

## `rate one`
Give a card a personal rating out of 10. Your rating gets factored into the card's overall community rating, so you're helping other players too!

---

## `rate remove one`
Remove your personal rating from a card.

---

## `rate many`
Rate multiple cards at once with the same score based on a query.

---

## `rate remove many`
Remove your ratings from multiple cards at once.

---

## `wish list`
View your wishlist — all the cards you're hoping to get! You can also check another player's wishlist by providing their user ID.

---

## `wish one`
Add a single card to your wishlist. If you already own the card it'll let you know, but you can still add it.

---

## `wish many`
Add a bunch of cards to your wishlist at once based on a query.

---

## `wish remove one`
Remove a single card from your wishlist.

---

## `wish remove many`
Remove multiple cards from your wishlist at once.

---

## `lock one`
Lock a single card to protect it from being sold, traded, or used in bulk operations. Great for cards you want to keep safe!

---

## `lock many`
Lock a bunch of cards at once. You'll see the full list before confirming.

---

## `lock remove one`
Unlock a single card.

---

## `lock remove many`
Unlock multiple cards at once.

---

## `train`
Train a card to earn it experience points and level it up! Cards need to reach certain EXP thresholds before they can be ascended. Has a 15-minute cooldown.

---

## `ascend`
Combine two identical-star cards that are both fully levelled to ascend one of them to a higher star level. Both cards need to be maxed out and you can only ascend up to 3★. The second card gets consumed in the process.

---

## `combine`
Sacrifice one card to transfer its EXP into another card of the same star level, helping it level up faster. The card being used as fodder gets consumed. Lottery cards can't be combined.

---

## `mark one`
Put a custom emoji mark on a card so it shows up next to its name in your card list. Ko-fi subscribers only. Leave the emoji blank to remove the mark.

---

## `mark many`
Put the same emoji mark on a bunch of cards at once based on a query. Ko-fi subscribers only. Leave the emoji blank to remove marks from all matching cards.

---

## `level progress`
Check how much EXP a specific card has and how far it is from being ready to ascend.

---

## `level list`
See an overview of all your cards that have at least some EXP progress, sorted by how close they are to being ready. Great for keeping track of what's almost ready to ascend!
