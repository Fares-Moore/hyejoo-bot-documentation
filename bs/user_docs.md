# Hyejoo Bot Command Guide

## `balance`
Shows how much currency you currently have — flakes, vials, and lemons. Also tells you how much your next card claim will cost and roughly how many cards you can afford right now.

---

## `inventory list`
Shows everything you have sitting in your inventory. Handy if you've forgotten what items you've picked up!

---

## `inventory use`
Use an item from your inventory. You'll get a confirmation prompt before anything happens. Note: if you have a legendary ticket, you'll need to use `/legroll` for that one specifically.

---

## `inventory sell`
Sell an item you don't need anymore for half its original price. Blueprints pay out in lemons, everything else in flakes. Bonus items can't be sold though.

---

## `inventory info`
Look up the details of a specific item in your inventory — what it does, what type it is, and any other info tied to it.

---

## `daily`
Claim your daily flakes! You can do this once every 20 hours (or 17 hours if you have the Ruler Jeanne effect active). The longer your daily streak, the more you earn. You'll also get fresh quests and a summary of anything going on in the bot — events, boosts, pending trades, and announcements.

---

## `journey`
Head out on a journey and come back with a random card! Has a 12-hour cooldown. You might get lucky and land something from the album collection too.

---

## `cards`
Browse through all the cards you own. You can filter and search to find specific ones. Each card shows its name, how many copies you have, any emoji mark you've set, and other details.

---

## `profile`
View your profile — or someone else's by providing their user ID. Shows your level, join date, daily streak, card collection size, and clout stats. If you're a Ko-fi subscriber, you'll also see a breakdown of your special cards and your set favourite card.

---

## `diff from`
Shows cards that another user has that you don't — great for figuring out what you could potentially get from them in a trade!

---

## `diff for`
Shows cards you have that another user doesn't — useful for knowing what you could offer them in a trade.

---

## `has`
Check if a specific card is in someone else's collection. Just search for the card name and their user ID.

---

## `miss`
Shows cards you're missing based on your search query. Great for tracking down what you still need to complete a collection.

---

## `quest list`
See all your active quests — daily, weekly, and monthly. Shows what each quest wants you to do, the reward, and how much time you have left.

---

## `quest info`
Get a more detailed breakdown of each of your active quests, including a guide on how to complete them and which commands to use.

---

## `stats`
Check out your activity stats across different time frames — today, this week, this month, and all time. Great for seeing how much you've been up to!

---

## `achievements`
See all the achievements you've unlocked. Use `missing:true` to see what you haven't gotten yet. Some achievements also grant special profile titles!

---

## `vote`
Shows the links to vote for the bot and lets you know if your vote is ready or still on cooldown. Voting gets you rewards like cards and flakes!

---

## `todo`
Not sure what to do next? This gives you a handy checklist of things to get done — daily claim, voting, claiming cards, completing quests, and collecting from your plots.

---

## `work`
Earn some resources every 10 minutes. Sometimes things go wrong and you come back empty handed, but most of the time you'll walk away with flakes, vials, or some user XP!

---

## `cooldown`
Quickly check how long until your commands are ready again — daily, work, train, journey, and kofi claim (for Ko-fi subscribers).

---

## `koficlaim`
Ko-fi subscribers only! Claim a free random lottery card every 24 hours as a thank you for your support.
